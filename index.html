How it works

Four steps. No new habits to learn.

You don't change anything about how you work. The system runs in the background and only bothers you when there's a real lead to act on.

01 /

You miss a call

Same as always — you're driving, on a job, or your hands are full. The call rings out, the way it does now.

02 /

They get a text within seconds

"Sorry we missed you! What's going on?" sent automatically, before they've had a chance to call the next business.

03 /

AI figures out what they need

It reads their reply, works out the job, and flags anything that sounds like a real emergency — like a burst pipe or no heat in winter.

04 /

You get one clear summary

Once there's enough to act on, you get a single text with the job and the number to call back. If the lead goes quiet, it follows up on its own.

Common questions

Straight answers.

Do I need a new phone number?

+
No. It can run alongside your existing number, or replace it entirely if you'd rather simplify down to one line. Either way works.

What if I already have voicemail set up?

+
Most people don't leave one. They just call the next business on the list. A text gets a reply. Voicemail mostly doesn't.

Can I cancel?

+
Yes, anytime. No contract, no lock-in, no cancellation fee.

Will it text spam calls and robocalls too?

+
It only reacts to missed calls, so the occasional robocall might get a text. It costs you nothing if they don't reply, and you can block repeat numbers if it ever becomes an issue.

How do I get notified about a lead?

+
A text straight to your phone — the same way you get texts now. No app to check, no dashboard to log into.

What if I miss a call after hours or on a weekend?

+
Same as during the day — the text goes out within seconds, any time, any day. It doesn't take nights or weekends off.

How long does setup take?

+
Most businesses are live within a day or two. We handle the setup — you just tell us a bit about how your business runs.
